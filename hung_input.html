Instruction:
 This mode aims to calculate volatile abundances in magmas using volatile compositions of apatite. i.e: F, Cl, H2O, and CO2 (optional).
The inputs of apatite compositions are reccommended to be measured directly.
Presures and Temperatures are also required for calculation where T plays larger role than P to get volatile estimates of low uncertainties.
Equation for calculating exchange coefficient (KD) and activity coefficient (Î³) were described in paper shown above.
